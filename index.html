What We Do

The jump between high school and university is a big one. There are hundreds of questions that you may ask: about different schools, whether the program is right for you, school-life balance, scholarships, student loans, and even residence life. We're here to help you with the transition. Speak with members from our diverse alumni network who have recently gone through the jump to learn the ins and outs of university life - all the things you can't find online.

Transition with First-hand Experience

Speaking with our alumni network can help you prepare for your next steps in your education. We help provide information on all aspects of student life including transportation, student financing (loans and scholarships), residences, employment opportunities, and even student perks.

Diverse Network

Our alumni networks spans almost every Canadian university, along with professionals studying in Europe, Australia, and the US. Learn about local information specific to your school of interest such as off-campus living and the best food deals.
